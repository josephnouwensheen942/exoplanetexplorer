Gas Giants

Description:Gas giants are massive planets primarily composed of hydrogen and helium, with relatively small rocky cores.
Characteristics:

  • These planets resemble Jupiter and Saturn in composition.
  • They typically have thick atmospheres and lack solid surfaces.
  • Gas giants can exist both close to their stars (hot Jupiters) or farther away, like Jupiter in our solar system.
    • Examples: HR 8799b, a massive gas giant orbiting a distant star.
