Neptune-like Planets(Mini-Neptunes)

Description:Mini-Neptunes are planets smaller than Neptune but larger than Earth, typically having thick atmospheres made of hydrogen and helium.
Characteristics:

  • These planets may have solid cores surrounded by thick atmospheres.
  • Their compositions are somewhere between rocky planets like Earth and gas giants like Neptune.
  • Mini-Neptunes are commonly found in exoplanet discoveries.
    • Examples: Kepler-452b, often referred to as "Earth's cousin."
