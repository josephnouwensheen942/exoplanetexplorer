Kepler 1811 b

Details about Kepler 1811 b, an intriguing exoplanet.

Planet Overview

Kepler 1811 b is an exoplanet that orbits a star located at a considerable distance from Earth. It is part of a fascinating planetary system that scientists are actively studying.

Key Facts

Discovery Year:2014

Distance from Earth:Approximately 1,200 light-years

Type of Planet:Super-Earth

Radius:1.5 times the radius of Earth

Orbital Period:8.8 days

Star:Kepler-1811(K-type star)

Potential for Life

The potential for life on Kepler 1811 b is still being researched, but it is of significant interest to astronomers.
